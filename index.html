The PLFI Token

A token used for governance and weighted indexes according to delegated stake.

As the index performs its high-tech functions and earns fees from growth, part of the revenue goes towards purchasing the token on the open market while removing it from the total supply indefinitely. As the DeFi space advances, the PLFI token becomes more scarce and valuable.

Plistfi Index Staking

PLFI LP Tokens – liquidity providers can stake their LP tokens and will be rewarded appropriately for their contributions to index health.

Plistfi features a two-tier staking model to reward participation in our index. Stakers can delegate their stake to any project in the index, signaling their support and increasing its weight. Through this design, PLFI captures innovation, community sentiment and the pulse of the DeFI space.
